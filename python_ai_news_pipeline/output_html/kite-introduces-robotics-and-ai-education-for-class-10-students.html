KITE Introduces Robotics and AI Education for Class 10 Students

Kerala Infrastructure and Technology for Education (KITE) introduces Robotics and AI education for class 10 students in the 2025-26 academic year, focusing on digital support for students with disabilities.

Jun 22, 2025Source: Visive.ai

Kerala Infrastructure and Technology for Education (KITE) has announced the integration of Robotics and Artificial Intelligence (AI) education into the curriculum for class 10 students during the 2025-26 academic year. This initiative aims to equip students with the skills needed to thrive in the digital age, with a special emphasis on providing digital support for students with disabilities.

KITE's Initiative for Class 10 Students

The program includes a series of district-level workshops for KITE nodal officers, which have been successfully completed. These workshops are designed to provide direction for the year’s activities of the Little KITES IT Clubs, student IT communities in public schools. The Little KITES IT Clubs will play a pivotal role in the implementation of this program, ensuring that students gain hands-on experience with the latest technologies.

Focus on Inclusivity

KITE CEO Anwar Sadath emphasized the importance of inclusivity in the program. Special focus will be given to students with disabilities, with the provision of digital support systems to ensure that all students can benefit from the new curriculum. This approach aligns with KITE’s commitment to making education accessible to all.

Workshop Participation

A total of 390 students from 196 units across the district participated in the workshop. The sessions and presentations provided valuable insights and direction for the year’s activities of the Little KITES units, ensuring that the program is well-coordinated and effective.

Future Prospects

The integration of Robotics and AI into the class 10 curriculum is a significant step towards preparing the next generation for the challenges and opportunities of the digital world. By focusing on practical, hands-on learning and inclusivity, KITE is setting a strong foundation for the future of education in Kerala.

The initiative is expected to have a positive impact on the overall educational landscape, fostering innovation and creativity among students. With the support of the Little KITES IT Clubs and the commitment of KITE, the program is poised to make a meaningful difference in the lives of students.

Conclusion

KITE’s decision to introduce Robotics and AI education for class 10 students is a forward-thinking move that aligns with the growing importance of these technologies in the modern world. By providing students with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed, KITE is helping to shape a brighter, more inclusive future for all.
